"No form of art goes beyond ordinary consciousness as film does, straight to our emotions, deep into the twilight room of the soul"

Even to the casual observer, Moviecuts prominence was clear.

Moviecuts is a bangalore based post production company focused on quality anecdote, striking visuals, and inventive design. The combined experience of the in-house team covers feature films, commercials, music videos, short films and web series.

The company consists of different departments: for post-production (editorial, visual effects, advertising, graphics) and original music. In recent years, movie cuts has made a diligent push into advertising and branding, producing theatrical trailers, movie title design, music videos and conceptual advertisements.
